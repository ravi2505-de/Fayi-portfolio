Animated Wedding Invitation

I was approached by a client to create a unique animated wedding invitation for a family member's ceremony. After initial discussions, I developed a concept in Adobe After Effects based on the animation flow they envisioned.

Client Requests Included:

Autumn-themed color palette
White floral accents
Tighter pacing and transitions
“Rainfall of envelopes” gift sequence
Text in Spanish with music

I presented three palette options. The client leaned toward earthy and pastel tones, so I avoided stereotypical fall reds in favor of muted natural colors.

Final adjustments included reversing name positions, fine-tuning typography, and adapting palette #2 into a pastel scheme.

These were the final flat versions approved by the client.

All graphic elements were crafted in Illustrator and animated in After Effects. The final product was well-received, and I learned valuable lessons about multilingual design and pacing in animation.
