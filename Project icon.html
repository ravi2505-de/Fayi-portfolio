Icon Study

This four-part study focused on a single object, exploring its form, abstraction, and symbolic translation. The phases included Elevation, Line Articulation, Graphic Translation, and Icon Finalization.

Elevation: A technical drawing capturing the structural form and proportions of the teapot from a side view.

Line Articulation: A minimalistic line representation of the same object from a new perspective, emphasizing contour and line quality.

Graphic Translation: Breaking down the object into simplified shapes while preserving recognizability and design rhythm.

Icon: Final exploration showing the finalized icon, plus alternate concepts and size variations — ready for real-world usage.
