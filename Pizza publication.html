Pizza Magazine Layout

The layouts were developed to highlight key recipes and promotions, combining mouth-watering images with engaging editorial design. Each spread was crafted to draw attention to featured dishes, seasonal specials, and upcoming events to maximize reader engagement and client impact.

I designed the layout for a pizza magazine for one of my clients, focusing on clean typography, appealing visuals, and balanced content placement. The aim was to ensure the magazine is both visually appealing and easy to navigate, catering to a diverse audience of pizza lovers and food enthusiasts.
